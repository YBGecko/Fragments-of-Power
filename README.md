# Fragments-of-Power
A minecraft modpack that combines my favorite modpacks together

## Why?
There are many modpacks out there, such as
- ATM 10
- RLCraft
- Vault Hunters
and many more that all scratch some kind of itch, but theres nothing with all of there capabilitys combined. Therefore Fragments of Power comes into play.

## The story
Skorvath Doomcaller, an evil sorcerer king who ruled the lands of Lepordosia. The shallow bones of a man who died many centries ago, who lives with immortality. He held a violent riegn killing anyone who threatened his power. You my friend, are a peasent who must make a living. In the land of this chaos where everything just wants to live, even the pigs. You must kill the king, you must build an empire, you must conqer and build the staff of blood, using the gems of the 9 great heros who got close. You my friend, must first mine, then craft your way to Skorvath Doomcaller.

## Massive changes to the game
Key features include but are not limited too
- Different eys of ender you must find to activate the end portal
- A healing overhaul using limb health instead of rows of hearts
- Passive mobs will fight back
- new mobs, structers, items, and dimensions!
- New bossess
- There is a level up system

## The vision
The world is suppost to be super sandbox like, open world, allowing you to do whatever you want. But the bug thing is Skorvath Doomcaller's questline to kill him and you "beat" the modpack. I want this to be mostly fatsasy themed, very little tech when needed (sophisticated storage) and to be really hard! That way its fun to explore, but also quite the challenge.
